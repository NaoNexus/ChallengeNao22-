Gipso NAO

Nao links our world with the ancient one.

In 2021, we had the amazing opportunity to participate in our first-ever NaoChallenge. This competition was a chance for us to showcase our technical skills and creativity in designing an application for the NAO robot. Our team decided to use the robot as a tour guide for a private art exhibition held within our school.

To make the experience even more special, we also developed a companion app that allowed guests to directly interact with the robot for a more personalized experience. This meant that the NAO could answer questions about the art and provide additional information. We wanted to make sure that each guest had a memorable and enjoyable time exploring the exhibition and learning about the art on display.

The development process was not without its challenges, but we persevered and were able to create an innovative and functional system that impressed our school community.
