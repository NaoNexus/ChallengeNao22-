What is the NaoChallenge?

NaoChallenge is a national-level robotics competition held annually in Italy. The event is centered around the humanoid robot NAO, designed and manufactured by Aldebaran Robotics, and is open to all high school students throughout the country. The competition provides an opportunity for young individuals to showcase their creativity, innovation, and teamwork skills by designing and developing unique robotics projects using NAO.

Innovation, Development and Creativity

these are the essence of the NaoChallenge.

The competition has gained immense popularity over the years and has become a platform to foster a culture of innovation and scientific exploration among the youth. The teams participating in the competition come from all over the country and strive to create the most innovative and unique projects. The competition provides a platform for these students to not only showcase their technical skills but also to learn from their peers and industry experts.

The competition has been successful in raising awareness about robotics and technology among young individuals in Italy. It has also helped in promoting the development of a skilled workforce in the field of robotics and automation. The NaoChallenge has become an influential event in the Italian robotics community and has inspired many young individuals to pursue careers in the field of robotics and automation.

Following our victory in the previous year's competition, we have decided to once again take on the challenge with a new project and new team members. This year's project will reach even greater heights thanks to the team's collaboration with Amperia, a well-known company in the eco-living world.

Project Green Nao

A smart robot for a better future.

NaoNexus presents GreenNao

Green Nao is a project that aims to highlight the eco-friendly infrastructures of our beautiful city, Verona. From the lush greenery of our parks and woods to the bustling urban streets, Green Nao showcases the associations and sustainable enterprises that have as a goal the protection of our planet in the interest of future generations. Whether you're a Verona resident or a first-time visitor, this project will leave you with a newfound appreciation for the beauty of our city and, hopefully, a deeper understanding of the urgent need to protect it.

The protagonists

In these episodes, Nao will introduce us to some of the most important eco-friendly infrastructures and cooperatives in the area. We will learn how these structures and cooperatives have been built to help local families and generate green energy. These eco-friendly infrastructures have been designed to reduce the impact on the environment while also helping to support the local community. By using renewable energy, these infrastructures and cooperatives can help to reduce the consumption of fossil fuels and promote a more sustainable future for all.

Moreover...

Green Nao also serves as a source of information on the latest measures taken by the city to address the issue of excessive air pollution. Through its regular updates, Green Nao aims to raise awareness among its audience about the importance of sustainable practices and encourage them to take action towards protecting the environment.
