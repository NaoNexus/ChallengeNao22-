NAO, A TEACHER STRIVING FOR AN ECOLOGICAL FUTURE.

Our project for the NaoChallenge 2023 is centred on the theme of eco-living, living ecologically. We chose this direction because we firmly believe in its actuality, considering the hot topic of climate change. This year we are supported by Amperia, an important company in the industrial sector of energetic eco-sustainability with ramifications throughout Italy. Their main objective is increase the efficiency and reduce the emissions of all types of structures through improvements to their electrical systems. Their expertise has allowed us to widen our goals, in the idea of achieving new feats in a technological field destined to grow exponentially in the future.

Us visiting the Amperia company

One of Amperia's solar panels

For this NaoChallenge, the NaoNexus team developed an interactive system aimed at analysing to the finest detail the energy consumption of a hypothetic client and, subsequently propose modifications that would render it more eco-friendly. Our plan is to make it applicable to any context, with a focus on schools and other academic institutions.

NAO in our computer lab

NAO in Amperia

At the fulcrum of this process is NAO. The humanoid robot analyses data with its numerous sensors, capable of detecting even the slightest variations in carbon dioxide, humidity and luminosity levels. Following this, it offers smart suggestions intended to improve the efficiency of the studied system. Thanks to a different sensor kindly donated by Amperia, our metallic friend is also capable of calculating particular statistics, such as the exact number of trees to plant to minimize one’s carbon footprint.

NAO’s analysis is then communicated to a potential user in the form of a dialogue with the robot itself. It is capable of answering to any type of question and give tips on possible actions that would improve the client’s situation. For example, NAO is able to specify the exact number of photovoltaic panels required to reduce to zero one’s emissions.

These objective might seem unreachable at first, but thanks to NAO and the system we created they quickly become achievable, if not reasonable. This project represents for us a new step towards a more eco-friendly Italy and a greener future.

Meeting with Amperia

On March 24th, 2023, the NaoNexus team traveled to Amperia to discuss the project and collaboration between the two groups. They were welcomed by Massimo Polato, CEO of the company, who not only helped the team refine their project but also provided relevant insights on clean energy.

The key points were experimentation, data collection, and awareness, as knowledge is necessary to optimize consumption and find suitable solutions for energy needs while avoiding its waste and unnecessary expenses. From solar communities to new technologies for producing and storing clean energy, the team left the meeting enriched with new and interesting information.

Feature on Radio Adige

On April 6th, our team had the opportunity to showcase our project on a local radio station called "Radio Adige". During the interview, we discussed our history, which led to our victory in last year's NaoChallenge, and highlighted our current project in collaboration with Amperia. This offered a preview of the upcoming NaoChallenge.
