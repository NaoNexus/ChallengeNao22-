What is the NaoChallenge?

The NaoChallenge is a national-level robotics competition annually held in Italy. The event is centered around the humanoid robot NAO, designed and manufactured by Aldebaran Robotics, and is open to all high school students throughout the country. The competition provides an opportunity for young individuals to showcase their creativity and teamwork skills by designing and developing unique robotics projects using NAO.

Innovation, Development and Creativity

these are the essence of the NaoChallenge.

The competition has gained immense popularity over the years, becoming a platform to foster a culture of scientific exploration among the youth. The participating teams come from all over the country and strive to create the most innovative and unique projects. It allows for these students to not only showcase their technical skills but also to learn from their peers and from experts in the field.

The NaoChallenge has been widely successful in bringing attention to robotics and technology alike, especially among young individuals in Italy. It is also appreciated for promoting the formation of skilled workers in the fields of robotics and automation, inspiring many to pursue related career paths.

Following our victory last year, we have decided to once again take on this challenge, with a new project and new team members, hoping to achieve even greater heights thanks a collaboration with Amperia, a well-known company of the eco-living world.

NaoNexus presents GreenNao

Green Nao is a project that aims to highlight the eco-friendly infrastructures of our beautiful city, Verona. From the lush greenery of our parks and woods to the bustling urban streets, Green Nao showcases the associations and sustainable enterprises that have as a goal the protection of our planet in the interest of future generations. Whether you're a Verona resident or a first-time visitor, this project will leave you with a newfound appreciation for the beauty of our city and, hopefully, a deeper understanding of the urgent need to protect it.

The protagonists

In these episodes, Nao will introduce us to some of the most important eco-friendly cooperatives in Verona. We will learn how these infrastructures generate their green energy and, at the same time, helping local families. How have they been designed to reduce the impact on the environment? How do they promote a more sustainable future?

Moreover...

Green Nao also serves as a source of information on the latest measures taken by the city to address the issue of excessive air pollution. Through its regular updates, Green Nao aims to raise awareness among its audience about the importance of having sustainable living practices, and to encourage to take action towards protecting the environment.
