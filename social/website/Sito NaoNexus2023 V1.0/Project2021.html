Gipso NAO

Nao links our world with the ancient one.

This year project for our first Naochallenge is based on the theme art and culture. The humanoid robot nao will be able to show and guide a tourist around the statues museum in the school Stimate. This will let people all around the world to discover and see art pieces never shown to the public before because inside a private structure. Though an app the robot is also able to turn its head in order to point the the art piece taken into consideration.
