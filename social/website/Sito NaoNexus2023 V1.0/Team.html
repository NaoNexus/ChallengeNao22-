Giovanni Bellorio

Giovanni Bellorio, also known as ‘er supremo’ is a professor at Stimate High School and the team leader. He is the one that made this project possible. Not only he is essential to coordinate all the students in the team and needs to approve every decision taken during this experience; but he also thought everyone of us everything we needed, in order to reach every year for something more difficult and challenging. His dedication and patience surely inspired us a lot during these months. We, as a team, can just be grateful for his presence in the team.
