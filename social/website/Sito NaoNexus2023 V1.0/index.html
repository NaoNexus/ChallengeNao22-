What is the NaoChallenge?

Naochallenge is the only challenge on a national level in Italy, open to every high school student, that gives the possibility to its partecipants to evolve their capabilities in the technical and programming filed, while also aquiring skills useful for life. Teams from all over the country challenge themselves to create the most innovative, unique and revolutionary project ever created with the use of the humanoid robot Nao.

Innovation, Developtment and Creativity

these form the essence of the Naochallenge.

Naochallenge is a national roboticâ€™s challenge that has as its own porpouse to connect humanoid robotics and the world that sourround us. Every year, groups from all over Italy, challenge themselves in the creative and technological field by partecipating to it. They have to work in the months before the challenge to a project where these two elements are combined to obtain great innovations in the field stated by the association. This year it will be sustainabiliy.

The main character of the challenge is the humanoid robot Nao designed to be able to interact with people. This is the media that every project needs to use to obtain the result wanted.

Nanonexus, after the great victory of last year, decided again to join the challenge with a new project and new team members. This year project will be taken to a even higher level thanks to the collaboration of the team with Amperia, a well known company in the ecoliving world.
