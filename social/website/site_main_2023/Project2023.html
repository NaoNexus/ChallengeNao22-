NAO, A TEACHER STRIVING FOR AN ECOLOGICAL FUTURE.

Our project for the NaoChallenge 2023 revolves around on the theme of eco-living, living ecologically. We chose this direction because we firmly believe in its actuality, considering the hot topic of climate change. This year we are supported by Amperia, an important company in the industrial sector of energetic eco-sustainability with ramifications throughout Italy. Their main objective is increase the efficiency and reduce the emissions of all types of structures through improvements to their electrical systems. Their expertise has allowed us to widen our goals, in the idea of achieving new feats in a technological field destined to grow exponentially in the future.

One of Amperia's solar panels

For this NaoChallenge, the NaoNexus team developed an interactive system aimed at analysing to the finest detail the energy consumption of a hypothetic client and, subsequently, propose modifications that would render it more eco-friendly. Our plan is to make it applicable to any context, with a focus on schools and other academic institutions.

NAO in our I.C.T. lab

NAO at Amperia’s headquarters

At the fulcrum of this process is NAO. The humanoid robot analyses data with its numerous sensors, capable of detecting even the slightest variations in carbon dioxide, humidity and luminosity levels. Following this, it offers smart suggestions intended to improve the efficiency of the studied system. Thanks to a different sensor kindly donated by Amperia, our metallic friend is also capable of calculating particular statistics, such as the exact number of trees to plant to minimize one’s carbon footprint.

NAO’s analysis is then communicated to a potential user in the form of a dialogue with the robot itself. It is capable of answering any type of question and give tips on possible actions that would improve the client’s situation. For example, NAO is able to specify the exact number of photovoltaic panels required to reduce to zero one’s emissions.

This objective of ours might seem unreachable at first, but thanks to NAO and the system we created it quickly becomes achievable, if not reasonable. This project represents for us a new step towards a more eco-friendly Italy and a greener future.

Meeting with Amperia

On March 24th 2023, the NaoNexus team traveled to Amperia’s offices to discuss on the project. They were welcomed by Massimo Polato, CEO of the company. He helped us refine our work and provided relevant insights on clean energy production and consumption.

The key points discussed were experimentation, data collection and raising awareness. The NaoNexus team and Amperia agree that, while the first two are undoubtedly important, especially on technical side of things, they are subordinate to the third. We both believe that in order to fix a problem, one must first understand it. Being familiar with one’s individual carbon footprint is necessary to find suitable solutions intended to reduce it. From solar communities to new technologies aimed at producing and storing clean energy, the team left the meeting a new, enriched perspective.
